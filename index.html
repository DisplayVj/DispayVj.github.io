ABOUT ME

Hello. My name is Vijay Kumar and I am a London based videogame designer and developer. I have a deep passion for the game development process as I strongly believe that videogames are a powerful medium for telling stories and for providing players rich, new experiences.

I specialise in level design and map creation, which requires me to study how the placement of entities, obstacles and story elements affect the overall player experience; though it is often overlooked, the structure and layout of a game has an immense influence on gameplay. My role as a level designer is to guide the player through a level, organically interlacing key game mechanics and story elements, whilst retaining a challenging atmosphere, in order to provide a satisfying and rewarding play. I sometimes draw inspiration from my childhood favourites, such as Contra and the TMNT series, which I use to motivate my own designs and layouts.

Overall, I am incredibly excited for what the future holds, for the videogames industry and my personal pursuits.

Dimmer

A 2D, top-down shooter designed using the cyberpunk aesthetic to create an immersive futuristic atmosphere. I created a level layout such that the placement of obstacles and enemies creates a progressive difficulty curve. I also assisted in the UI design and tried to provide a comfortable experience for players.

Life Is A Beach

Made for the 2021 Brackeys Game Jam, 'Life is a Beach' has the player sneaking around a Hawaiian resort causing absolute chaos. When designing the level, I had to take into consideration the stealth elements of the game, providing plenty of rooms, corners and walls for the player to hide behind. The game uses a cartoonish, voxel style which reflects the comedic premise of the game.

Two Worlds

Using Unity Pro-builder, I designed the map for a 3D platformer with two overlapping, super-imposed worlds in one level. I employed an increasing difficulty curve to provide a more entertaining gameplay experience. This project was made in 48 hours for the GMTK game jam 2021, which had the theme "Joined Together".
